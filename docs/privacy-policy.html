Privacy Policy

Skimpulse - What's Hot from Skimfeed

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Skimpulse ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Skimpulse does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Skimpulse, certain information may be automatically collected:

  • Device Information: Your device type, operating system version, and device identifiers may be collected by third-party advertising services (see Section 3 below).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as which articles you view, may be collected by advertising services.
  • Network Information: Your IP address and network connection type may be collected by advertising services.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To analyze app usage patterns (anonymously)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

Skimpulse uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and app usage to provide personalized ads. This information may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or iOS Identifier for Advertisers)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Location data (general, not precise)

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

3.2 Content Sources

Skimpulse aggregates content from Skimfeed.com. When you click on articles, you will be directed to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Skimpulse is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about what data we have collected.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising through device settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 8.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
